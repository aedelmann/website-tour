Hola, we're Alex & Cristyne

We're a couple who swapped a fixed address for a rolling one. Alex is German, a lifelong tech geek, solution architect, and full-stack engineer who has spent 20+ years building cloud and IoT systems. Cristyne is from Singapore and a fashion designer—she graduated from LASALLE College of the Arts in Singapore and most recently led fashion products at Heinemann in Singapore for the South-East Asia and Oceania region. She's also our chief adventure planner, photographer, and the reason we always find the best tapas bar in town.

Together we're exploring Europe and beyond in our {{ .Site.Params.tesla_model }}, documenting the places, the costs, the bureaucracy, and the occasional gale-force balcony wind along the way. We started in southern Spain and we're not in a hurry.
